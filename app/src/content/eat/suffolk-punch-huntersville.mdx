## Why newcomers love it

Suffolk Punch's Huntersville location opened in December 2025 and
immediately became the downtown's new daytime-into-evening anchor —
full-service coffee bar until afternoon, scratch kitchen at lunch and
dinner, a tap list in the evenings. The South End original is a
reference point most newcomers already know; the Huntersville version
brings that weekday pattern to a town that was short on a good
third-place. Family-friendly by design; dogs on the patio welcome.
