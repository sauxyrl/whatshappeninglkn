## Why newcomers love it

A casual Cornelius breakfast anchor that earns its line. Hot cheddar
biscuits get named by nearly every local review; the eggs Benedict
converts people who say they don't order eggs Benedict. Come before 9
on a weekend morning or come after 10:30 — between those is a wait.
Good family-friendly fit on a post-Jetton-Park Saturday.
