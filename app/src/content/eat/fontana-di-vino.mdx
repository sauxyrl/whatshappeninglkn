## Why newcomers love it

Davidson's newest dining-room anchor, opened December 2025 in the
building that used to house Davidson Ice House. "Modern pasta
chophouse" is how the owners describe it — fresh pasta program,
steaks, a full cocktail bar downstairs that's its own draw. Too new
for reviewers to have converged on a signature dish; the early read
from locals is that the room delivers on the building's atmosphere.
