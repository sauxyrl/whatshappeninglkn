## Why newcomers love it

Downtown Mooresville's best dining room and the easiest argument that
Mooresville has a dining scene worth driving to. Set inside a
circa-1888 building on Main Street a block from the Walk of Fame. Menu
is a traditional chophouse — 30-day aged certified USDA choice beef,
seafood, pasta — plated in a room that dresses up the way people want
on an anniversary. Reservations wise on weekends.
