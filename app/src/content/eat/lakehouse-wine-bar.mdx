## Why newcomers love it

The big 13,500-square-foot waterfront room that used to be Port City
Club, reopened under new ownership as LakeHouse Wine Bar & Grill. The
boat-dock count (twenty-two) is more than any comparable LKN
restaurant, and the indoor dining area is one of the few that keeps
its lake view through winter. Menu runs wide — small plates, steaks,
seafood, house pasta — and the wine program is the differentiator
against its lakefront peers.
