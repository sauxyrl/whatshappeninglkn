## Why newcomers love it

The Kindred family's lake-side room — same Joe-and-Katy team as Kindred
in Davidson, different tone. Calabash-style seafood served from a
retro-chic dining room perched right at Holiday Marina. In the Michelin
Guide for Cornelius, which is a fair signal for the food even if the
vibe reads more Friday-night-lake than hushed-room. Boat slip available
if you're arriving by water. Reservations help on weekends.
