## Why newcomers love it

The casual waterfront option at Davidson Landing, a short bike ride
from Main Street if you're local or a straight shot off I-77 Exit 30
if you're not. Nine boat slips for guests; a patio that catches the
sunset on a good evening; a menu that runs American-and-seafood
without overreaching. Book a patio table — the dining room is fine,
but the view is the whole reason you came.
