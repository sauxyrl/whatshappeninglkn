## Why newcomers love it

The restaurant a Davidson local names first when a Charlotte visitor
asks where to eat. Husband-and-wife team Joe and Katy Kindred opened it
in 2015; Bon Appétit named it one of the best new restaurants in
America that year, and Joe has since been a James Beard semifinalist.
The menu rotates with what's in from farms and boats, but the milk
bread is the dish that never leaves. Ask for a bar seat if it's two of
you on short notice; reserve for four or more. Closed Mondays.
