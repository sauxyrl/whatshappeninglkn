Mooresville wears the "Race City USA" branding because NASCAR's
headquarters economy lives here. For most movers, that's background; the
practical pitch is different: Mooresville has the biggest grocery, retail,
medical, and recreational footprint north of Charlotte. You can live your
life here without driving 15 miles for the mundane errands that define
day-to-day.

The cost is the commute. Mooresville is the outer ring of the Charlotte
labor shed — if uptown or south Charlotte commute is part of your life,
add 10–15 minutes vs. Cornelius.

Real version of this page talks about MGSD vs. Iredell-Statesville schools
(Mooresville splits districts), the difference between the lake-front
neighborhoods and the inland subdivisions, and how the NASCAR community
shapes local retail more than most newcomers expect.
