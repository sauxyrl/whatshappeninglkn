## Overview (REQUIRED — 200–400 words)

Huntersville is the town the rest of this guide can be hardest on by
accident: writers gravitate toward Cornelius's lake access or Davidson's
walkability and skip it. That's unfair. For most families relocating from
Charlotte or out of state, Huntersville is the most pragmatic choice on
the east side of the lake — more housing inventory, closer to uptown, and
the daily rhythm is easier to plan around.

The trade-off is that it's not a lakefront town. You drive to the water
from Huntersville. For some families that's a deal-breaker; for others
it's a fair price for a yard they can afford.
