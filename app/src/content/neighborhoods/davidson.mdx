Davidson is the town that most newcomers describe as "where I'd move if I
could find a house." The reason is the college: Davidson College's
walkable downtown, free-to-public events, and a Main Street that has
resisted becoming a strip of chain storefronts give the town a character
the rest of the lake doesn't quite have.

For families, the pitch is the school district and the Davidson Day
option. For young professionals, it's the cafes, a bookstore that's still
open, and a weeknight ecosystem that doesn't require driving to Charlotte.
For retirees, it's the walkability plus lake-adjacent access (though
Cornelius beats it on pure lake proximity).
